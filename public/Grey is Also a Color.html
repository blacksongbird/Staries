Sinopsis

Breonna is an 18 year old girl whose mom passed away 6 months ago. Living only with her father, she is diagnosed with High-Functioning Autism. Just after finding out about her disorder, Breonna meets Grey; a human representation of her mind's autism. Since then, everything in her life just becomes gray.
